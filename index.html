Chocolate Cake

"This easy recipe for chocolate cake is a classic. It will please even the most indulgent."


Difficulty Perparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Preparation

1

 Melt the chocolate with the butter

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and flour

3

 Mix everything to make a paste

4

 Bake
